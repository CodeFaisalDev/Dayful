Dayful Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Our Data Promise

Dayful is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on any servers. Your routines, habits, and tasks stay on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Explicitly: Dayful does not collect any user data.

We believe that your personal routine and habits are yours alone. Therefore:

  • No account creation is required to use Dayful.
  • No personal information (name, email, phone number) is requested.
  • No location data is tracked.

2. How Data is Stored

All the data you enter into Dayful—including your tasks, habits, routines, and categories—is stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. None of this information is transmitted to our servers or any third-party databases.

If you delete the app or clear its data, your routines and progress will be lost as we do not maintain any cloud backups.

3. Third-Party Services

To support the development and maintenance of Dayful, we use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as your device's advertising ID, to show relevant ads. You can review AdMob's privacy policy here.

4. Device Permissions

Dayful may request permissions to enhance your experience:

  • Notifications: To remind you of your scheduled routines and habits.
  • Local Storage/Media: To save your app preferences and routine data locally.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
